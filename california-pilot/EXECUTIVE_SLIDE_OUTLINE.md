---------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 1 — THE PROBLEM (CURRENT STATE)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

TITLE:
"Pay & Chase Oversight Is Expensive and Late"

DIAGRAM:
[ Vendor Claim ] ---> [ Payment Issued ] ---> [ Audit Months Later ]

KEY POINTS:
- Payments issued before reconciliation
- Audits and clawbacks are slow and costly
- Disputes are narrative-driven
- Public trust erodes when corrections come late

EXECUTIVE TAKEAWAY:
Oversight happens after money moves.


---------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 2 — THE SHIFT (PROPOSED STATE)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

TITLE:
"Verify & Release Oversight"

DIAGRAM:
[ Vendor Claim ]
        |
        v
[ Reconciliation ]
        |
        v
[ Payment Released ]

KEY POINTS:
- Reconciliation happens before disbursement
- Errors are prevented, not chased
- Faster, cleaner settlement

EXECUTIVE TAKEAWAY:
Oversight moves upstream.


---------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 3 — HOW RECONCILIATION WORKS (TRIPLE-ENTRY)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

TITLE:
"Three Independent Records, One Settlement"

DIAGRAM (TRIANGLE):
  Vendor Claim (A)
        ▲
        |
        |
Client Receipt (B) —— Network / Meter (C)

KEY POINTS:
- A: Vendor asserts delivery
- B: Counterparty confirms receipt
- C: Independent physical / telemetry meter
- All three must reconcile

EXECUTIVE TAKEAWAY:
No trust required — only matching facts.


---------------------------------------------------------------------

---------------------------------------------------------------------

TITLE:
"Automation Handles the Majority of Cases"

DIAGRAM:
[ A + B + C Match ] ---> [ Auto-Settlement ]

KEY POINTS:
- Clean records settle automatically
- No human intervention
- Faster payment cycles
- Reduced administrative load

EXECUTIVE TAKEAWAY:
Humans are not involved unless something breaks.


---------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 5 — WHAT HAPPENS WHEN RECORDS DON’T MATCH
---------------------------------------------------------------------

TITLE:
"When Automation Stops, Humans Decide"

DIAGRAM:
[ Mismatch Detected ]
        |
        v
[ Escrow Freeze ]
        |
        v
[ Ring-0 Human Decision ]

KEY POINTS:

EXECUTIVE TAKEAWAY:
Automation pauses instead of guessing.


---------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 6 — RING-0 (HUMAN BACKSTOP)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

TITLE:
"Ring-0 Is Rare, Slow, and Intentional"

DIAGRAM:
[ Evidence Review ] -> [ Binary Decision ] -> [ Attestation Logged ]

KEY POINTS:
- Human answers one question: True or False
- No partial settlements
- Written rationale required
- Decision is append-only and reviewable

EXECUTIVE TAKEAWAY:
Human judgment is preserved — not automated away.
