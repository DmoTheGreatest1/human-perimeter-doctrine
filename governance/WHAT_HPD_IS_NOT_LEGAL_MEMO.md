Human Perimeter Doctrine (HPD)
"What HPD Is Not" Legal Clarification
=====================================================================

Purpose: Defensive clarity for counsel and risk review

---------------------------------------------------------------------
HPD Is NOT a Regulator
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD does not:
- issue rules
- enforce compliance
- impose penalties
- mandate behavior
- override statutory authority

---------------------------------------------------------------------
HPD Is NOT a Vendor
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD does not:
- sell software
- provide services
- host infrastructure
- operate systems
- charge licensing fees

---------------------------------------------------------------------
HPD Is NOT Surveillance
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD does not:
- collect raw traffic
- inspect payloads
- monitor individuals
- require continuous telemetry
- exfiltrate operational data

---------------------------------------------------------------------
HPD Is NOT Punitive
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD does not:
- fine vendors
- claw back funds
- blacklist participants
- escalate violations
- infer intent

---------------------------------------------------------------------
HPD Is NOT Binding Law
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD:
- creates no legal obligations
- replaces no statutes
- supersedes no contracts
- compels no action

---------------------------------------------------------------------
HPD Is Documentation
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD provides:
- reconciliation structure
- decision boundaries
- evidence documentation
- audit visibility

Institutions decide how records are used.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Funding Clarification
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD does not:
- set prices
- negotiate compensation
- collect payment
- enforce funding

Institutions resolve funding internally.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Risk Posture
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD reduces risk by:
- making decisions explicit
- anchoring responsibility
- limiting discretionary drift

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Conclusion
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD documents governance.
It does not exercise it.
