Human Perimeter Doctrine (HPD)
Executive Overview
=====================================================================

Audience: Governors, Agency Heads, Commissioners
Length: One Page

---------------------------------------------------------------------
What This Is
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD is a governance framework that improves how institutions reconcile
money, performance claims, and reality.

It preserves human authority while reducing administrative friction.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
What Problem It Solves
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Public programs often suffer from:

- slow verification
- adversarial audits
- delayed payments
- unclear accountability
- political friction around disputes

HPD addresses these without enforcement.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
How It Works (Plain English)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

- Automation handles routine reconciliation
- Discrepancies pause the process
- Humans resolve only the rare, high-impact cases
- Decisions are documented and permanent

No penalties. No surveillance. No clawbacks.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
What Changes
---------------------------------------------------------------------

- Faster settlements
- Clearer records
- Fewer disputes
- Reduced audit burden
- Better planning data

---------------------------------------------------------------------
What Does NOT Change
---------------------------------------------------------------------

- Legal authority remains with the state
- Agencies retain full control
- Vendors are not punished
- No new regulatory powers are created

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Cost Posture
---------------------------------------------------------------------

- Reading and discussing HPD is free
- Operational reliance is institution-funded
- Pricing and compensation are determined by the state
- HPD does not invoice or collect

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Why This Is Safe
---------------------------------------------------------------------

- Non-proprietary
- Fork-safe
- No vendor lock-in
- No enforcement authority
- Exit at any time

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Bottom Line
---------------------------------------------------------------------

HPD allows governments to move money based on evidence rather than
argument, without expanding bureaucracy or regulatory reach.
