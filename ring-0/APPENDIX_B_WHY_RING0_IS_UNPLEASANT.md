Ring-0 is intentionally:
- Stark
- Slow
- Binary
- Stressful

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY THIS IS NECESSARY
---------------------------------------------------------------------

High-stakes systems fail when humans can hide behind process,
automation, or ambiguity.

Ring-0 removes those shelters.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
COMMON OBJECTIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------

"Add a third option."
No. Negotiation destroys determinism.

"Make it more accessible."
Accessibility does not mean comfort.

"Let AI assist decisions."
AI may assist analysis, not judgment.

"Reduce stress on officers."
Stress is a signal of responsibility.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
POSITIVE FUNCTION
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Unpleasantness:
- Filters misuse
- Prevents casual fraud
- Forces attentiveness
- Preserves legitimacy

---------------------------------------------------------------------
CONCLUSION
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The unpleasantness of Ring-0 is not a flaw.
It is the mechanism.
